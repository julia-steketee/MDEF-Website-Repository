I left during the first part of the 2nd half of the day. We were divided into spectators and actors. Hearing from the classmates the following day. The next step was to assign a role to each member of the acting team. Next they were to organize themselves in a human sculpture or system-scape that illustrates the connections between each element of

  1. the higher education system as it is today
  2. the transition that the higher education system is going through
  3. an imagined higher education system that this group would like to see in the future


Mapping our local Ecosystem





What's our vision?



to explore the possiblities of how mycelium can be used as a tool to

  1. manage and digest our waste in a sustainable and natural way.
  2. build bio-based structures that have a negative carbon impact and promote community cooperation and stewardship.
  3. increase awareness on how to grow mycelium and what practical applications it could be used for.
  4. start conversations and promote education around bio-based solutions to challenges that our societies face today.
